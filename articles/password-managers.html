VPN and Password Manager Recommendations

Why Use a VPN with a Password Manager

1. VPN Protects Your Password Security - VPN encrypts your internet connection, safeguarding your privacy when using password managers and ensuring your password data isn't intercepted by hackers during transmission.

2. The Role of Password Managers - Password managers securely store and manage multiple passwords, preventing the use of repetitive or simple passwords and enhancing account security.

3. Top Password Manager Recommendations

• 1Password：High security, supports multi-device synchronization.

• LastPass：Clean interface, powerful free version.

• Dashlane：Excellent auto-fill and password generation features.

• Bitwarden：Open-source and highly secure, suitable for tech-savvy users.
